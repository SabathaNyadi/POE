Abalindi welfare society openly welcomes donations that would help us further our project and help those who are in need in our community, every contribution big or small helps us continue our mission to make an impact to peoples lives so please help us change peoples lives You could help by:

    Donating food and clothes
    Volunteer time
    Cash donations

When donating you can make it clear on which project you are choosing to donate to with Old Age Home, Thokozani Creche and Childrens home being your three options
