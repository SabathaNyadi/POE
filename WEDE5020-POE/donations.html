Donations

Abalindi Welfare Society openly welcomes donations that would help us further our projects and help those who are in need in our community. Every contribution, big or small, helps us continue our mission to make an impact on people's lives, so please help us change lives.

You could help by:

  1. Donating food and clothes
  2. Volunteering time
  3. Cash donations

When donating, you can make it clear on which project you are choosing to donate to, with Old Age Home, Thokozani Creche, and Children's Home being your three options.
