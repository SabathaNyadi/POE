Abalindi Welfare Society

Abalindi Welfare Society is a non-profit organization established in 1970 in the Amatikwe community, based in Durban, KZN. The organization primarily focuses on people over the age of 60 who have been abused or not taken care of by their family members. After a few years, they decided to include children in the society, aged 0–18 years old, referred by district social workers, for the society to care for them. The organization showed its success and decided to create a creche which has also become successful. Abalindi Welfare is a very Christian-centered organization, to an extent that they host a Seventh-day Adventist church every Saturday, which is Amatikwe SDA church. They also welcome the Children of the Sea home and Abalindi Elders to come.

Abalindi Welfare Society has ventured into different business opportunities such as creating a well-known bakery in the area in order to gain funds for the organization. They further created a Creche known as the Thokozani Creche where the community could take their children to learn, and they provide them with aftercare services.

This initiative created job opportunities for the community which made the organization realize how jobs were needed in the community. So they decided to open:

  1. Block making project
  2. Sewing project
  3. Emery Farming

The initiatives at Abalindi Welfare Society focus on income generation and maintaining ongoing projects (Abalindi Welfare, 2022).

HIV/AIDS epidemic

With the HIV/AIDS epidemic which has signficantly effected the black community the Abalindi welfare society decided to launch a support program, in this program people who are effected directly or indirectly by HIV/AIDS are supported by recieving conseling, support group and it encourages those who are affected by it to join the programs that are offered by us This program also invites parents who have lost their children due to HIV/AIDS, helping them deal withe thier loss and provide a community around them. (Abalindi welfare,2022)

Vision

“To provide preventative and institutional services for the benefit of the aged, disabled and children in need of social therapeutic and development care for all people”

Mission

“ Abalindi mission is to reach out to all communities in KZN promoting The well-being of the elderly persons by providing comfort dignified treatment, health needs accommodation to those have a need Well-being to children who are destitute, orphaned or abandoned protecting them from any abuse promoting their schooling always preparing them for a better future, providing a residential facility for those needing it.”
