Our Services

Abalindi offers a variety of services, from helping people of a very young age to a very elderly age. From education to health, it is our biggest goal to help people in whatever way we can. Below you will find different ways we do that:

Old Age Home

Abalindi Welfare society provides alot of services in the organization such as taking care of the elderly by providing health care facilities, personal care , accommodation and many activities that can help them with their health

Childrens Sea Home

Childrens sea home caters for children at the age of 0-18 years,Abalindi Welfare provide accommodation,health care facilities and Education in nearby schools we also offer activities such as soccer and netball to the kids to keep themselves busy.

Thokozani Creche

Abalindi welfare offer a Daycare centre called the Thokozani Creche they offer this service to the community it is one of the forms they generate funds for the programs that they have also helping the children to get the necessary education needed.
